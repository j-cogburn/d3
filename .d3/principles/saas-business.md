# SaaS business
**Consulted at:** DEFINE (success metric) · LEARN

- **NRR > 100%** — net revenue retention; top performers ~104-106%.
- **LTV:CAC > 3:1** — healthy unit economics.
- **CAC payback < 12 months** — efficient acquisition.
- **Gross margin 70-85%** — SaaS-grade margins.
- **Rule of 40** — growth rate + profit margin >= 40%.
- **PLG + activation in first 7 days** — drive first measurable value fast; self-serve compresses CAC.

**Gate:** objectives that move revenue tie their success metric to one of these.
